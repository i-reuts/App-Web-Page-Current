Description

Search & Scan

Type in the product name or barcode to get specials for this and similar items in grocery stores around you. Search items and compare their prices by simply scanning the barcode. Click on the star icon to add an item to your favorites list.

Favorites

Save time by adding frequently purchased items to your favorites list. Quickly check whether some of your favorite products (or articles similar to them) are currently in special in grocery stores nearby.

Built-in map

Display all grocery stores in your area using an integrated map tool.

Store Location

Click on any product in your list to get the product details and display on the map the location of stores where this product is currently in special.
